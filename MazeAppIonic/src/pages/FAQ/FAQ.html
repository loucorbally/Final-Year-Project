Frequently Asked Questions Winter Level Summer Level Farm Level General Questions

Does the elf follow always?

Yes the elf will consistenly follow you around maze once you are in its sight.

How many coins do I have to collect?

There are 5 coins in this level, the coin counter is on the top right hand side of the screen.

How long do I have to collect coins?

The player has 60 seconds to navigate the maze & collect the coins.

Why is this a beach level?

We decided that each level should have a unique theme which influences how the environment will be created and what assets are made for the scene.
Each level has a different theme.

How many enemies are there?

The number varies per level, this level the crab is considered an enemy.

How many lives do i have?

You only get one life per level.

Can I drive the tractor?

Unfortunately not, the tractor is for display purposes only.

Will the animals chase me?

No, the animals on the farm won't chase you. They like people!

Is it difficult to get out of the maze?

Perhaps, but isn't that all part of the fun.

What if I don’t make it out before the timer runs out?

When the player has collected all of the coins or runs out of time in the timed levels they will be redirected back to the hedge maze hub level.

How many levels are there?

There are three main levels, however to make the gaming experience more understandable and interesting from the player’s perspective we added a hub level which ties all
of the levels together.
The hub level is where the player begins the game

What platforms is the game available on?

At this current moment the game is only available to download on PC.
